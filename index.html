ABOUT ScapeSync

A data-responsive media facade for living landscapes.

SCAPESYNC converts visitor photographs into site-specific photomosaic media art inspired by local landmarks.
Real-time environmental and urban data reshape color, light, motion, atmosphere, and nature-inspired patterns
so each visit becomes a responsive public artwork.

DESIGN

Regional landscapes, reshaped by live data.

SCAPESYNC blends photomosaic composition, regional imagery, 3D landscape modeling, procedural animation, shaders, and particle systems to turn familiar scenery into a responsive public experience.

TECHNOLOGY

A synchronized pipeline for photos, data, and display geometry.

SCAPESYNC collects visitor photos and site-relevant environmental or urban data, synchronizes and normalizes inputs with different formats and update cycles, then maps them into visual parameters for real-time photomosaic rendering.

TECH_01

Layered Spatial Depth

Foreground and background layers, camera framing, dynamic lighting, atmospheric depth, and volumetric effects create scale and continuity.

DEPTHCONTINUITY

TECH_02

Display-Aware Layout

Composition adapts to screen resolution, aspect ratio, curvature, viewing distance, and geometry for flat, curved, circular, and facade displays.

TECH_03

Parametric Visual Mapping

SCAPESYNC translates place data into controllable visual parameters: tile density, object masks, particle behavior, color rhythm, and motion timing.
